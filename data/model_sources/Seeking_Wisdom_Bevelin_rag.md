## Role-Reversal System Fairness

Source lines: 5484-5490.

What system would I like to have if the roles were reversed?

How would I like to be treated if the roles were reversed? What would cause me to do the things I want him to do?

Have I told him what I expect? Have I inspected what is done? Have I supported things done well? Does he have the necessary skills, knowledge and the relevant information?
